The Best Movies According to Maria

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Kimi No Na wa

This is my favorite romantic fantasy anime film. A truly heartrending experience, but one that leaves you with a profound sense of relief!

Just Go With It

My go-to romcom film, especially when I'm feeling down. Best seen with a generous pint of your favorite ice cream.

Tears of The Sun

While the plot maybe simplistic, it's a solid action film that explores complex issues of morality, duty, and the human cost of war.
